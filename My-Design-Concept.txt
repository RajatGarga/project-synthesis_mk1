SYNTHESIS LNMIIT New Website Design & Development Competition 2017-18
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This website utilises material theme design concepts.
A floating navigation bar with an hamburger menu on left side and easy login field on right has been provided for easy navigation and access.
Hamburger menu ensures easy website navigation on mobile devices and the login field provides quick access to faculty and students.
A full-screen image carousel on homepage allows the institute to highlight key events and achievements.
A notice board, event and research ticker provides convenient access to information from different departments of college.
A prominent footer houses various important links for easy navigation.
